Dhaal Pay - UPI Security & Financial Insights

Ensuring secure digital payments and financial health through UPI ID verification and CIBIL score tracking.

Overview

In the fast-growing digital payment landscape, Unified Payments Interface (UPI) has revolutionized transactions in India. However, with its convenience comes a significant challenge‚Äîfraudulent transactions and unverified UPI IDs. Users often struggle to determine whether a UPI ID is legitimate, leading to security risks and potential financial losses.

Dhaal Pay is a mobile application designed to address this issue by enabling users to verify UPI IDs before making payments. By providing crucial insights like UPI account authenticity, transaction frequency, location data, and security status, Dhaal Pay empowers users to make informed decisions while ensuring a safer digital transaction experience.

Problem Statement

With the rise of UPI-based digital payments, fraud cases have also increased significantly. Many users unknowingly send money to fraudulent or newly created UPI IDs, leading to financial loss and security concerns. The lack of transparency in UPI transactions prevents users from verifying recipient authenticity before transferring money.

Additionally, many individuals struggle with financial health management, often unaware of their CIBIL score and creditworthiness. A low CIBIL score can:

  • Reduce loan and credit card approval chances.
  • Increase interest rates on loans.
  • Impact financial stability and future borrowing power.

Ideation

The ideation phase started by identifying the main pain points our target users faced:

  • Security: Ensuring UPI transactions were safe from fraud.
  • Financial Literacy: Helping users track and improve their CIBIL scores.
  • Simplified Finance: Offering tools to manage bills, loans, and payments.

User Feedback Integration

UPI Verification: Feedback from users like Rahul and Priya revealed a major concern about authenticating UPI IDs before payments. This led to the development of a UPI verification system.

CIBIL Tracking: Many users like Ramesh wanted a simple way to track their credit score. We integrated clear, actionable CIBIL insights into the app.

Financial Reminders: Users requested a payment reminder feature to avoid missed bills and improve their financial discipline. This was incorporated as a high-priority feature.

Feature Evolution

Initial Concept: Initially, the app only offered basic UPI payment verification.

User Feedback: After conducting user testing, we realized that integrating CIBIL score tracking and financial tips would address broader user needs.

Final Concept: The app evolved to include fraud alerts, expense tracking, and personalized financial insights to help users take proactive control of their finances.
